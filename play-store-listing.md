## Full Description (4000 chars max)

NoGoon is a screen-time tool that actually works — not by shaming you, but by creating a moment of pause.

When you open an app or website you've chosen to block, NoGoon intercepts it with a quick mini-game. Not a lecture. Not a guilt trip. Just a 30-second reset that lets your brain catch up to your fingers.

WHY IT WORKS

Most cravings pass in 90 seconds. NoGoon fills that window with something engaging — a pattern memory challenge, a breathing exercise, a typing test — so by the time you finish, the urge to scroll has faded. You decide what happens next.

WHAT YOU GET

• 8 mini-games: Stroop Challenge, Pattern Memory, Typing Test, Grounding Exercise, Breathing Reset, Why Am I Here, Reaction Speed, and Odd One Out

• Block any app or website — Instagram, TikTok, Reddit, Twitter, YouTube, or anything else on your list

• Intentional Day streaks — track how many days you've walked away instead of scrolling

• Brick Mode — for when you mean it. Removes the unlock button entirely. Play the game, then you're done.

• Points system — earn points for every mini-game you complete. Walk away and earn bonus points.

• Accountability partner — optionally add someone who gets notified about your milestones

• Random mode — NoGoon picks a different game each time to keep you on your toes

HOW IT WORKS

1. Set your blocklist — pick the apps and sites that waste your time
2. Enable the Accessibility Service — this lets NoGoon detect when you open a blocked app
3. Go about your day — when you open something on your list, NoGoon pops up with a mini-game
4. Play the game, then decide — after the game, choose to walk away (earn points) or continue

NO GUILT. NO SHAME.

NoGoon doesn't track how much time you "wasted." It doesn't show you scary screen time numbers. It just creates a speed bump — a moment where you can choose to be intentional instead of automatic.

PRIVACY FIRST

• The Accessibility Service runs entirely on your device — we never see which apps you open or websites you visit
• We only store whether you walked away (a yes/no event for your streak)
• No data selling. Your journal entries stay private.

FREE DURING LAUNCH

All Pro features are free during the launch period. Every game, every mode, no restrictions. Try everything and see what clicks.

BUILT FOR REAL PEOPLE

NoGoon was built by someone who needed it. Not a Silicon Valley productivity app — just a simple tool that makes the scroll a little harder and the pause a little easier.

Break the scroll. Build the habit.
