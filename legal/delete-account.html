Delete Your Account

NoGoon respects your right to control your data.

If you would like to delete your NoGoon account and all associated data, you can do so by following the steps below.

How to Request Account Deletion

  1. Send an email to the address below from the email associated with your NoGoon account.
  2. Include the subject line: "Delete My NoGoon Account"
  3. We will verify your identity and process your request within 30 days.

Send your request to:

jtshibao@gmail.com

What Data Gets Deleted

When your account is deleted, we permanently remove the following data:

  • Your account profile (email, display name, avatar)
  • Your blocklist (blocked apps and websites)
  • Your journal entries
  • Your mini-game history and scores
  • Your streak records and point balance
  • Your accountability partner settings
  • Your subscription status (note: active subscriptions must be cancelled separately through Google Play)

What Happens to Your Subscription

Deleting your NoGoon account does not automatically cancel your subscription. If you have an active subscription, please cancel it through Google Play before requesting account deletion to avoid being charged after your data is removed.

Data Retention

After processing your deletion request, all personal data is permanently removed from our systems within 30 days. Anonymized, aggregate data that cannot be linked back to you may be retained for analytics purposes.

Questions?
